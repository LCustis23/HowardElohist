About 

Welcome to the Howard University Elohist Club. Our mission is to share the love of God to all students and testify about God Elohim-- God the Father and God the Mother. We are a Christian, non-denominational group with the sole focus of wanting to learn the TRUTH of the Bible. We Encourage all Howard Students to come learn this truth, and join us on the path to the Kingdom of Heaven. As Jesus said: "‘Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.’” (Matthew 28:19), so we teach the New Covenant and the original truth Jesus taught.

Events 

The Howard University Elohist Club is comprised of 2 parts: IUBA and ASEZ. The goal of IUBA is to create a haven for students who are interested in True God to learn all the truths recorded in the bible. We create an environment where the members can stay close to the word of God in a daily life. ASEZ makes efforts to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals spearheaded by the United Nations, through worldwide environmental campaign. ASEZ will actively cope with climate change and environmental issues as well as organize events and activities to address pressing needs in communities around the world. As future leaders, ASEZ unites students, communities and governments to create real and lasting change through the SAVE Movement. The work of ASEZ takes place in more than 175 different countries. .
